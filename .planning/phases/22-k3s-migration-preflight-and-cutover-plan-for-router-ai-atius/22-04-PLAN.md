## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| T-22-04-01 | Denial of Service | Apache cutover | high | mitigate | Backup vhost, configtest before reload, keep Podman active, rollback script. |
| T-22-04-02 | Information Disclosure | public `/v1/models` | high | mitigate | Smoke forbidden fields and Go-owned payload shape. |
| T-22-04-03 | Tampering | automated cutover | high | mitigate | Checklist script cannot edit Apache automatically; manual checkpoint required. |
| T-22-04-04 | Reliability | rollback | high | mitigate | Validate rollback before and after cutover. |
</threat_model>
